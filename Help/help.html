SHOPPING GUIDE

On Zara Home you can purchase either as a GUEST or as a REGISTERED customer.

Registering is very easy and your details will always be available on your account.

If you purchase as a guest, you will find all the information in the confirmation email.

We will send you a confirmation email with all the details after placing your order. You can only request the cancellation of your order if it is not already in an advanced status of preparation.

You can always request an invoice for your purchase and you can purchase as an individual or as a company. You will have access to the invoice by downloading it from your account once the order is shipped out.

All our items have the same price online and in store.

You can use the following payment methods: VISA, MC, AMEX PAYPAL

Your purchase will be charged when your order is shipped out but if you have paid with PayPal the payment will be charged at the moment of the purchase.

You can shop with full trust, we take great care in providing resources that guarantee the security of your purchases and your data.

Home delivery fees are 45 EUR.

The estimated delivery time will be between 7-10 working days.

Please bear in mind that some of our deliveries may be affected by import costs out of our control. We will let you know if these costs are included in the final amount of the purchase or need to be paid when receiving the order. If the second case applies, we suggest that you get in touch with your local Customs Office for further information.

You will receive email updates about the status of the delivery of your order and if you have purchased as a registered customer you will be able to check its status on your account too.

You have 30 days from the shipping date to return your purchase. Return costs are 45 EUR and they will be deducted from your refund.

TO REQUEST A HOME RETURN:

If you have purchased as a registered customer, you should access your order information from your account and request the return.

If you have placed the order as a guest, request the return through the link included in the confirmation email.

Once your return request is registered, you will receive an email with the return instructions and the return labels to prepare your package. Please, follow the instructions to avoid any issues.

Remember items must be in the same condition you received them in.

You will receive an email confirmation of the return once it is completed. The refund will be made to the same payment method used to pay for the order.
